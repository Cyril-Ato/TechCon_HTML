History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate technologists. Over the years, it has evolved into a globally recognized event, bringing together the brightest minds in software development, AI, cybersecurity, and emerging tech. From its humble beginnings in San Francisco, TechCon has now hosted over 50,000 attendees and featured 300+ renowned speakers.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, connect communities, and push the boundaries of what technology can achieve. We aim to foster a culture of collaboration, inclusion, and continuous learning. Each year, we spotlight emerging trends and celebrate the pioneers shaping the digital future.

Notable Past Speakers

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Alphabet and Google, delivered a keynote at TechCon 2016 focused on the evolution of AI and its impact on accessibility. His leadership in cloud computing and Android development has shaped the way billions interact with technology.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, the CEO of Microsoft, joined TechCon 2018 to share insights on digital transformation in the enterprise sector. Under his leadership, Microsoft embraced open source, cloud-first strategies, and accessibility-first design.

Fei-Fei Li

Dr. Fei-Fei Li, a leading AI researcher and Stanford professor, presented at TechCon 2020. She discussed the importance of human-centered AI and ethical development in machine learning. Her work on ImageNet has been foundational to modern computer vision.
